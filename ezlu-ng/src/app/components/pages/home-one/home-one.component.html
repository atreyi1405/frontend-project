Our Powerful Features

Each service provided is developed with your best interest in mind. Explained briefly below are some of TranscriBot's most powerful features. Click on Services to learn more about all the services in detail.

Speech Analytics and Discrepancy Analysis

We provide detailed analytics about Client-Employee interaction and their speech analysis, thereby identifying areas of improvement.

Language Detection and Translation

We help bridge the language barrier between you and the customers that might not be native speakers by detecting and translating the language.

Speech to Text and Text to Speech Conversion

In addition to translation, we also provide back-and-forth text to speech or speech to text conversion for an effortless connect with the customers.

Audio and Text Sentiment Analysis

Using Sentiment Analysis, we give high importance to your customers and know just what you need for positive customer experiences.

Background Noise Cancellation and Quality Enhancement

One of our many powerful features also includes noise cancellation which enhances the quality of Client-Employee interaction.

High Rate of Call Acceptance and Voicemail Support

TranscriBot helps free up the QA that further ensures that most if not all your calls are accepted, we also support voicemail option.
